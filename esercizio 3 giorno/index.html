Focaccia whit Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

How you taste buds whit this festive focaccia appetizer topped whit sweet caramelized onion, tart granberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday stater

Featured in: fresh cranberry recipes that go beyond sauce

Ingredients

for the focaccia

  • 4 cups(500) all-purpose flour
  • 1 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g)lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus morefor the pan

for the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions,thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cube (scrabe rind off whit a paring knife if desired,thought it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranbarries

Method

  1. make the focaccia dough

    2. in a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer whit a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbe. Tou don't need to actually knead the dough, thought I sometimes knead it a few timesin the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the lat of the flour
